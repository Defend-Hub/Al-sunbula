Our Mission

We at Al-Sunbula Charity Association believe that goodness is a spirit that runs through the veins of the nation, so we strive to be a hand that revives hearts before bodies, and a light that blossoms from our Islamic values ​​to illuminate the paths of the deprived.

Our mission is to create true empowerment that transforms need into independence, poverty into dignity, and despair into hope, through:

  • Planting a culture of sustainable giving that builds and does not consume, and develops and does not depend.
  • Strengthening the values ​​of social solidarity with a spirit of faith that reminds us that generosity is worship and nature.
  • Building humanitarian partnerships that transform charity into a bridge between hearts, and make charitable work a life project, not a passing season.
  • Protecting the dignity of beneficiaries through initiatives that preserve Their humanity, and restores their confidence that they are partners in construction, not just numbers in a statistical record.
  • We believe that every step we take with kindness and skill is a seed of goodness that produces a nation based on mercy, and dominated by the human dignity that God has honored. Our goal is not to give a hand, but to light a candle that illuminates the path of man to himself and to his Lord

Our Vision

To be a permanent mark of goodness in the hearts of humanity, illuminating paths of hope, and building bridges of giving, to reach a world in which human dignity is preserved, societies are developed with the tolerant values ​​of Islam, and the rights of the weak are protected with hearts filled with faith and solid institutional work

Dedicated to making a positive impact in our community through sustainable charitable initiatives.
